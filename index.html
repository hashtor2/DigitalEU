The Invisible Web

Why is deleting your Gmail account so terrifyingly hard?

When you sign up for subscriptions, utilities, newsletters, or online shopping, you use your email address. Over the course of 5–10 years, your old mailbox accumulates an invisible web of accounts.

If you press "Delete Google Account" today, you lose the ability to recover passwords or verify purchases for those accounts forever. You are locked into Google because tracking down 50+ registered services is an administrative nightmare.

⚠️ Google's Retaliatory Deletion Friction:

Google purposefully scares you when you try to leave, showing long warning sheets of lost services to ensure you remain tracked in their advertising profile.

Our Solution

The Pain-Free Migration Process

We've designed a systematic 3-step workflow that takes you from a bloated, insecure Big Tech mailbox to complete private independence.

1

Local Inbox Scan

Connect your Gmail/Outlook via secure OAuth. Our system pulls headers only and scans them 100% locally inside your browser. Your email content never leaves your computer, and we never store your passwords.

2

Direct-Link Navigation

No more looking around. For every service discovered in your inbox, we provide a direct deep-link to that service's specific "Change Email" settings page, saving you thousands of clicks.

Autofill & Update

Our Chrome/Firefox extension detects when you arrive on the change-email page, automatically generates a secure alias or suggests your new Proton/Tuta mail, and fills it in instantly. One click and you're done.
